Instructions

How to play :

1. Register yourself first by clicking 'Join Hunt' at homepage.

2. Login by clicking 'Take Flight' at homepage and use your Team Name (Case-Sensitive) and password given at the time of registration.

3. You start as a young Mothra with level 'born'.

4. Click on 'Go Hunting' to attempt the next challenge.

5. You are given a puzzle. You have to solve it to find the correct answer.

6. Write the answer and submit.

7. The answer will be all small letters and spaces need to be replaced with an underscore. For example if answer is 'Hello World', you need to submit it as 'hello_world'.

8. The number of attempts are infinite.

9. You can get a hint of a particular stage only after 1 hour of reaching that stage. You have to refresh to update the time remaining.

9. If your submission is accepted then you are upgraded to the next level and can attempt a new challenge.

Judging Criteria :

1. There are a total of 7 upgrades in the hunt.

2. Your level in the hunt is the last stage you cleared.

3. You need to reach the highest level as soon as you possibly can.

4. The team to reach a higher level before others will be ranked higher.

5. The tie break is Time itself. A person reaching a higher stage even by a millisecond will be ranked higher.
